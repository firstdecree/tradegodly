Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TradeGodly. We take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we handle any information you provide while using our trading journal platform.

Our core mission is to provide a secure environment where traders can reflect on their performance without compromising their sensitive financial data.

2. Data Collection

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services. This includes:

• Account information (Username, Hashed Password)
• Trading information (Date, Asset, Type, Status, Returns, etc.)
• Strategy details and trading rules

We do not store your raw passwords; they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (SHA512) before being saved to our database.

3. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the trading journal experience. Specifically:

• To generate performance statistics and analytics.
• To provide AI-driven insights into your trading behavior.
• To maintain your session and secure your account.

We never sell your data to third parties.

4. AI Processing

Our AI features use anonymized trade data to provide performance summaries and advice. This data is processed through secure API endpoints. We do not include personally identifiable information (PII) in prompts sent to AI cores.

5. Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information:

• AES-256 Encryption: Sensitive session data is encrypted using high-level AES-256 standards.
• Secure Cookies: Cookies are set with HttpOnly and SameSite attributes to prevent XSS and CSRF attacks.
• Data Isolation: Your trading history is tied to your account and cannot be accessed by other users.

6. Use of Cookies

TradeGodly uses cookies exclusively for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out to us via our community Discord channel.
